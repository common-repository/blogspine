== Description ==

The BlogSpine widget for Wordpress allows you to display your blogrolls listed in your BlogSpine account. This is very helpful to manage blogrolls when you have multiple blog sites.

== Installation ==

1. Unzip 'blogspine.php' file to your '<blog directory>/wp-content/plugins/' directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress Admin.
3. Go into Appearance->Widgets and drag the BlogSpine Widget onto your sidebar.
4. Login to your BlogSpine account. Copy your "Blog Key" that shows on your blogroll list table and paste it to your BlogSpine wordpress widget configuration.
